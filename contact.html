Restaurant History

Our restaurant was established in 1990, and since then we have been offering the best dishes to our customers. We started as a small restaurant in the heart of the city, and over time, we have expanded to become one of the most famous restaurants in the area. We take pride in offering traditional dishes with a modern touch, always ensuring to use the best fresh ingredients.
